Dandelion Creek Co.

Welcome to our Soapery

Step off the beaten path and into the heart of Plattsburg, Missouri, where we craft small-batch soap from the milk of our own Alpine and Saanen goats. Named 1st Place in the 2025 Best of Rural Missouri, our charming farm shop has become a beloved stop for travelers and locals alike.

Handcrafted Goat Milk Soap

Free of harsh chemicals and detergents, every bar is hand-poured, cut, and stamped on-site, using fresh goat milk, nourishing oils, and carefully chosen ingredients. Whether you're sensitive to scents or love a bold fragrance, there's a bar for you.

Meet the Goats

Each morning, we're up with the sun milking our sweet herd of goats — they're the real stars behind every bar of soap we make. That fresh milk goes straight into our small batches, made right here at the shop. It's part of our farm-to-skin promise: simple, local, and made with care (and maybe a little goat gossip).

If you visit in the spring, you might even catch our baby goats bouncing around behind the shop and maybe even snag a turn bottle-feeding one — it's pure joy, topped with loads of goat kisses.

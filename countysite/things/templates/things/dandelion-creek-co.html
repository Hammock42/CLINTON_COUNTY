Welcome to Dandelion Creek

Handcrafted Goat Milk Soaps from Farm to Your Skin

Step off the beaten path and into the heart of Plattsburg, Missouri, where Dandelion Creek crafts small-batch soap from the milk of their own Alpine and Saanen goats. Named 1st Place in the 2025 Best of Rural Missouri, this charming farm shop has become a beloved stop for travelers and locals alike.

Every bar is hand-poured, cut, and stamped on-site, using fresh goat milk, nourishing oils, and carefully chosen ingredients. Whether you're sensitive to scents or love a bold fragrance, there's a bar for you.

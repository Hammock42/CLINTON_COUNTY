Cameron Veterans' Memorial Golf Club

Golf in the Country

Golf courses seem more at home in the country than in the city. No highway noise in the background. No long lines at the tee boxes. No snobs in the pro shop. All of which explains why Cameron Veteran's Memorial Golf Course is such a pleasure to play.
Our course features stimulating layouts without the high greens fees you pay for city courses and tee times are easier to acquire.

This course has a motto: "Manage your approach shots and our well-maintained greens will reward those players who have mastered the art of putting."

Plattsburg Country Club Golf Course

Golf in the Country

Golf courses seem more at home in the country than in the city. No highway noise in the background. No long lines at the tee boxes. No snobs in the pro shop. All of which explains why Plattsburg Country Club Golf Course is such a pleasure to play.
Our course features stimulating layouts without the high greens fees you pay for city courses and tee times are easier to acquire.

This course has been challenging golfers since 1921. Its par 71 layout is 6,250 yards of unique holes designed by Jeff Klaiber, a former Pete Dye architect. It rolls through hills, across a couple of creeks and around ponds. The fairways are bluegrass; the undulating greens are bentgrass.

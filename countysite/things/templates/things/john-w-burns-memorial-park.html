John W. Burns Memorial Park

All-Inclusivefun for every body in Lathrop, MO

Welcome to John W. Burns Memorial Park, home to Lathrop's all-inclusive playground and the Variety KC Splash Pad. Built through years of community fundraising and partnerships, the destination features an estimated 10,000 sqft of inclusive play space with rubber surfacing, ramped structures, sensory-rich play, and an accessible splash pad — designed so kids and caregivers of all abilities can play side-by-side.

What makes it Inclusive?

The playground and splash pad were designed with guidance from inclusion advocates to remove common barriers: wide ramps for mobility devices, poured-in-place surfacing, transfer points, group spinners and swings, and water features reachable from seated positions. The space encourages multigenerational play—kids, parents, and grandparents can participate together.

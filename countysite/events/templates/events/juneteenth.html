Juneteenth

We celebrate Juneteenth as only a small town can—a slice of history served with a dollop of neighborliness. We gather on the lawn of the Clinton County Historical Society in Plattsburg to celebrate our commonalities rather than our differences with picnic fare, music and conversations. Costumed Buffalo Soldiers give history lessons. Barbecue, greens and slices of pie lay spread on a makeshift buffet table.

Rev Your Engines for Clinton County's Classic Car Shows

Clinton County is a top destination for small-town car shows, where classic cars and vintage beauties take center stage. Lathrop hosts one of the area's oldest shows, now in its 46th year, every mid-June, drawing entries from across the region. Contact Lathrop Antique Show at info@lathropantique.com for more details.

During Gower Days on the second weekend in July, car enthusiasts can enjoy Gower's lively annual show. And in Plattsburg, don't miss the car show on the fourth Saturday in May.

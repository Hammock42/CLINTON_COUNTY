Riley-Carmack House Ghost Hunt 2025

When the sun goes down, the spirits come out to play! Join us for an unforgettable night of paranormal investigation at the historic Riley-Carmack House in Plattsburg, Missouri. This event is perfect for thrill-seekers and history buffs alike.

The Riley-Carmack House, a Victorian (140 years old) landmark turned museum, has long been home to whispers of the unexplained. Many stories tell of friendly spirits who linger in its halls. Guests report soft touches, faint voices, and unseen companions. But not all who enter feel at ease. Paranormal investigators once fled before dawn — unable to finish the night. Some say a darker presence hides beneath the surface, waiting to be discovered.

During this after-hours event, you will:

  • Hunt for spirits with provided EMF detectors, UV flashlights, and dowsing rod.
  • Conduct your own paranormal investigation in small groups.
  • Hear chilling tales of guests and investigators who met the house's ghostly residents.
  • Decide for yourself—are they truly friendly… or something else?

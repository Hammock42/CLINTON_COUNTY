Celebrate Small Town Festivals in Clinton County
Nothing captures the spirit of rural America like a small-town festival, and Clinton County is home to some of the best!
Lathrop Friendship Festival

Join Lathrop, "The Home of Friendly People", for its 61st annual Friendship Festival on May 29-31, 2025. Experience frog races, pie-eating contests, bathtub races, carnival rides, live music, and more! From BBQ and food trucks to carnival treats, there's something to satisfy every craving. More Info

Cameron's Independence Day Celebration

Celebrate July 4th in style with Cameron's red, white, and blue weekend on July 3-5, 2025. Enjoy a patriotic concert by the city's band, plus an art show, bake sale, farmers' market, and countless vendors around McCorkle Square.

Gower Days

Held the weekend after the Fourth of July, Gower Days is packed with family fun, carnival rides, live entertainment, and delicious food. The highlight? A car show featuring classic and modern beauties from around the region. More Info

Plattsburg Fall Festival

End the festival season with Plattsburg's Fall Festival on October 9-11, 2025. The event features local food, a lively parade, carnival rides, and a beer garden with live music at the Community Courtyard. It's small-town charm at its finest! More Info

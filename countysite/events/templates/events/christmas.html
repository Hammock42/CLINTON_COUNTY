Clinton County Christmas
Clinton County does Christmas the way it used to be—old fashioned and heartfelt. So get away from the bustle of Christmas in the city and experience the holiday season in traditional rural American style. How about a night parade with lighted Christmas floats crafted in barns and pulled by tractors and pickups? How about beautifully decorated downtowns with shop windows glowing with colored lights? How about hay rides, Christmas homes tours and homemade craft items for sale?
If you want to sample Christmas that has a rural America flavor, join us in Clinton County.

Plattsburg—Community Christmas, December 6-7, 2024. Friday afternoon at the Community Courtyard kicks off with local crafts and vendors, chili and homemade soups at the annual Forever Christmas event. Free train rides wind through Plattsburg neighborhoods and Santa shows up at the Backyard entertainment venue. The evening peaks with a spectacular lighted parade that arrives downtown at 7 p.m. Saturday the fun continues with the Forever Christmas Bazaar from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. At the same time, Plattsburg offers a sumptuous tour of homes. Five of its historic homes open their doors to show off their sparkling Christmas decorations. For more info: Click Here

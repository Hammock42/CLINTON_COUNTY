Cameron's Patriot Ride 2025

Cameron's UTV Patriot Ride attracts scores of riders and spectators every year to an event that benefits the Missouri Veterans Home. Utility vehicles wind through a 50-mile course of trails, gravel roads and city streets that ends at the Missouri Veterans Home. Over the years, sponsor Cameron Veterans Assistance League, has purchased four wheelchair-accessible vans for residents of the veterans' home. The League also uses funds from the Patriot Ride to pay for social events, televisions, benches and landscaping.

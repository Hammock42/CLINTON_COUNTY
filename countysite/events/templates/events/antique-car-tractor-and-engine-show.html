Lathrop Antique Car, Tractor, & Engine Show

If you're a gearhead or just like the pop-pop-pop sound of old tractors, the Lathrop Antique Car, Tractor, & Engine Show is the event for you.

The annual event is held on Father's Day weekend (June 12-15, 2025), at the Lathrop Antique Show Grounds. Dozens of old time tractors are the stars of the show. Co-starring are antique and collectible cars.

Enjoy classic events including a cross-country antique tractor cruise, a tractor pull on the fairgrounds, a pony pull and a draft horse pulling contest. There will be 10 different types of demonstrations, including one of the largest antique engines shows in the region, kid's pedal power tractor pull and live music.

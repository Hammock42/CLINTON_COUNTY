Cameron keeps its own rendezvous with the dead at an annual late summer cemetery tour presented by the Cameron Historical Society and Depot Museum. The group says, Visitors "line up behind Jeff Mitchell's hearse at the Depot Museum" and drive to one of Cameron's cemeteries where they "travel in a faux-funeral procession" to hear about the town's "unsolved mysteries involving some of its residents".

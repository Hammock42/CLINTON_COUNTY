Clinton County Fair

Nothing captures the charm of rural America like the Clinton County Fair. It's an endearing blend of farm families, farm animals, and fun as 4-H kids and their prized critters compete for blue ribbons and pride. For spectators, the county fair is a unique chance to glimpse farm culture firsthand and to savor barbeque, shaved ice and cold drinks served outdoors under sunny summer skies.

In Clinton County every June, the action takes place at the livestock pavilion just east of Plattsburg on Hwy. 116. Dozens of 4-H kids finally get to show animals they have spent months raising, training and grooming. The kids parade their charges before keen eyed judges as parents and grandparents anxiously watch. Only the finest of the lot come away with blue ribbons, and you can see pride glowing on the winning youngsters' faces.

Clinton County Golf Clubs
Golf in the Country

Golf courses seem more at home in the country than in the city. No highway noise in the background. No long lines at the tee boxes. No snobs in the pro shop. All of which explains why Clinton County's two public golf courses are such a pleasure to play.
Our courses feature stimulating layouts without the high greens fees you pay for city courses and tee times are easier to acquire.

Plattsburg Country Club

This course has been challenging golfers since 1921. Its par 71 layout is 6,250 yards of unique holes designed by Jeff Klaiber, a former Pete Dye architect. It rolls through hills, across a couple of creeks and around ponds. The fairways are bluegrass; the undulating greens are bentgrass. New ownership has taken great pride in making the course a joy to play, and it is also memorably scenic. For more information: https://www.plattsburgcc.com/golf/

Cameron Veterans' Memorial Golf Club

This course has a motto: "Manage your approach shots and our well-maintained greens will reward those players who have mastered the art of putting." At 5,809 yards from the black tees, the course accommodates wide skill levels. Water hazards on most holes add to the challenges. For more information: https://www.camerongolfclub.com/course-info/

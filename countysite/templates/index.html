Discover Clinton County, Missouri

— Your Perfect Rural Getaway!

Welcome to Clinton County, Missouri, where small-town charm meets endless adventure! Nestled in the heart of rural America, just up the road from Kansas City, we invite you to step into a world where history, nature, and community come together to create unforgettable family experiences.
Feel the Pulse of the Heartland
Stroll through our lively main streets, where festivals fill the air with music, laughter, and community spirit. Enjoy the charm of our famous wine walks, lighted Christmas parades and frog-jumping contests. For a one-of-a-kind experience, head to Shatto Milk Company, where you can see firsthand how farm-to-table works in Missouri. Enjoy fresh dairy products straight from the source, meet the cows, and experience a day on the farm—a perfect family adventure.
In Clinton County, tradition isn't just celebrated—it's a way of life.
Step into History
Explore our delightful Victorian-era homes and historic buildings that enrich Clinton County's towns. Take a walking tour of historic homes in Plattsburg and visit our museums where we celebrate northwest Missouri's rich rural past. Wander Lathrop's unique fairgrounds, home to interactive displays that tell the story of our 19th-century farming heritage. Relive the good old days at classic car shows that pay tribute to the glory of American automobiles, surrounded by folks who share your passion for vintage wheels and open roads.
Scenic Wonders & Outdoor Fun
Clinton County's serene landscapes make it a haven for nature lovers. Kayak the clear waters of Smithville Lake, where miles of shoreline offer perfect spots for picnicking, fishing and hiking. Stride the walking trails at Wallace State Park and the Nature Conservancy's Trice-Dedman Woods. Enjoy our peaceful countryside, where rolling hills and vibrant sunsets create a stunning backdrop for a relaxing day in Nature.
A Place of Peace
Make a pilgrimage to the serene Abbey of Benedictines of Mary, Queen of Apostles in Gower, which has become a tranquil spiritual destination, renowned for its beautiful Gregorian music and miraculous story. It's an unforgettable place, perfect for reflection and retreat.
A Community that Welcomes You with Open Arms
Clinton County isn't just a place—it's a feeling. We invite you to experience the warmth of our small-town hospitality and our spiritual connections that are rooted in kindness, respect, and shared values. Here, visitors quickly become friends, and friends become family. We can't wait to welcome you!

Abbey of Benedictines of Mary, Queen of Apostles
Seek Spiritual Refuge

Discover a unique place of spiritual harmony at the Abbey of the Benedictines of Mary, Queen of Apostles near Gower, Missouri. Nestled among peaceful farms and woodlands, the Abbey offers visitors a rare blend of faith, serenity, and marvel. Attend Mass and stroll the beautiful grounds where the sisters live a life devoted to prayer, work, and extraordinary music.

Music & Miracles

If you visit during Mass, you may hear the sisters' renowned liturgical choir, which earned Billboard's Classical Traditional Artists awards in 2012 and 2013 for their beautiful albums. The Abbey gained further attention in 2023 when the body of its founder, Sister Wilhelmina, was found intact after four years in the grave—a phenomenon some call miraculous. That summer, over 15,000 pilgrims visited the Abbey, drawn by faith, hope, and the search for grace.

Join Us at the Abbey

Visitors are welcome to view the body of Sister Wilhelmina and experience the peace of the Abbey. For more information and Mass times, please visit the Abbey's website. Out of respect, the sisters kindly request that visitors wear modest attire and honor private areas of the grounds.

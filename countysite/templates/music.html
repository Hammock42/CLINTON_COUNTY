Clinton County Comes Alive with the Sound of Music
From rock 'n' roll to country and gospel, Clinton County has a rich live music scene that offers something for everyone. Whether you're looking for a local festival vibe or an intimate performance at a cozy venue, Clinton County is the place to be.
150 Years of Music Tradition

For more than 150 years, the Cameron Municipal Band has delighted audiences with marches, show tunes, and popular hits. Their summer Concert in the Park series kicks off on Memorial Day at the Thomas J. Price Memorial Band Pavilion in McCorkle Park and continues every first Thursday of the month through August at 8 p.m. Enjoy free admission, old-fashioned charm, and treats from the Cameron Historical Society, which sells ice cream, cake, and soft drinks at the event.

Festival Fun and Live Music

Local festivals in Gower, Lathrop, and Plattsburg each feature live music as part of their annual celebrations. Don't miss the tunes at Gower Days (second weekend in July), the Lathrop Friendship Festival (last weekend in May), and the Plattsburg Fall Festival (second weekend in October). These events bring the community together with music, fun, and family-friendly activities.

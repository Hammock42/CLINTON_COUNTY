Shatto Milk Company
Experience Farm Fresh Fun

Wholesome Americana comes to life at the Shatto Milk Company in Osborn, Missouri. This family-run dairy farm, cherished by locals and visitors alike, offers an authentic glimpse into farm life.

Take the Tour

On a Shatto tour, you'll meet the cows, see milk bottling in action, and visit friendly farm animals like calves, goats, rabbits, and Bailey, the lovable farm dog. Kids and adults alike enjoy sampling Shatto's unique milk flavors, cheeses, and ice cream while hearing the story of a farm that's been proudly family-owned for generations.

Main Street Stroll

Imagine a sunny Saturday in May, strolling the cordoned off streets of a charming small town, a sample glass of wine in one hand, a delectable from a food truck in the other. If that's your idea of a good time, come to Plattsburg the third Saturday in May for the town's annual Main Street Stroll.

What started as a Wine Stroll has blossomed into a full celebration featuring local and regional wineries, craft breweries, and distilleries. Whether you're a fan of wine, beer, or spirits, you'll find your favorite sip along Main, Second, or Locust Street.

If you prefer shopping to sipping, enjoy Vendors' Row, where local artisans showcase handmade crafts, early spring plants, and unique finds. A DJ keeps the energy lively, and dance lovers are invited to show off their Two Step moves. With live music at venues like The Backyard and a tasty array of food truck options, there's something for everyone.

Come stroll with us in Plattsburg and enjoy the best of small-town charm, great drinks, and good company!

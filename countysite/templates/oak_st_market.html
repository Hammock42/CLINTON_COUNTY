Discover Local Treasures at Oak Street Market

Located in the heart of downtown Lathrop, Missouri, the Oak Street Market is your go-to destination for farm-fresh produce, handmade goods, and artisanal crafts. Running every 2nd and 4th Saturday from May through October, this vibrant market offers a variety of local delights, including fresh meats, baked goods, jams, jewelry, and unique crafts.

Whether you're looking for seasonal fruits and vegetables or one-of-a-kind creations from local makers, Oak Street Market has something for everyone. Visit from 9 a.m. to 1 p.m. and enjoy the friendly atmosphere while supporting local farmers and artisans.

Stay updated by visiting Oak Street Market on Facebook for the latest news and events!
